Membership

Membership policies | About Membership

Membership in our botanical garden offers individuals and families the opportunity to support our conservation efforts, access exclusive programs, and enjoy a range of benefits. Members will enjoy unlimited admission for the duration of their membership, early access to seasonal exhibits, discounts on special events, and exclusive invitations to members-only events. Membership is available in several tiers, including individual, family, and corporate options, with different pricing structures based on the level selected. All memberships are valid for one year from the date of purchase, with automatic renewal available unless otherwise specified.

Payments can be made through various methods, including credit cards and online options, and members may cancel their membership at any time by following the outlined cancellation process. Members are expected to adhere to all garden policies while visiting, including respect for our living collections and following safety guidelines. We take the privacy of our members seriously and ensure that personal information is used responsibly and securely.
