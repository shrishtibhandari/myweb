About the Teams

Formula 1 racing teams represent the pinnacle of engineering, innovation, and teamwork in motorsport. Each team is a powerhouse of talent, combining expert drivers, world-class engineers, and precise strategists to compete at the highest level. Legendary teams like Ferrari, Mercedes, and McLaren carry rich histories and passionate fanbases, while newer contenders like Red Bull Racing and Aston Martin bring fresh energy and cutting-edge technology to the grid. Every season, these teams push the limits of performance, constantly evolving their cars to gain even the slightest edge over the competition. Behind every race win is a symphony of coordination, where every decision — from tire strategy to pit stop timing — can make or break a weekend.
